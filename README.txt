The Generic Fish-Eat-Fish Game!
-------------------------------

Open main.py and run it in order to start the game.

Background:
	You are a rainbow fish, the last of your kind.
	You must quickly wipe out all other fish out there -
	either you eat them... or they eat you...

	Eat smaller fish to grow. Larger fish will eat you.

Controls:
	Control your fish using:
	- The arrow keys
	- WASD if you're familiar with those
	Esc will quit the game at any time
